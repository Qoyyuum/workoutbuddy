Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does WorkoutBuddy support?
WorkoutBuddy runs on Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, Linux, and the web.
Is WorkoutBuddy free?
Yes, WorkoutBuddy is open source and free to use.
How do I log my food and workouts?
Use the app's intuitive interface to add food entries and workout logs. See the README for more details.
Can I customize the app?
Yes, you can adjust settings and personalize your experience.
Who maintains WorkoutBuddy?
The app is maintained by Qoyyuum and contributors on GitHub.
